Privacy Policy

Last revised: January 1, 2026

The Silent Hour is designed to be a calm, story-focused reading experience. We respect your privacy and are committed to keeping this app simple, safe, and transparent.

What Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not ask for accounts, logins, or registration. We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, passwords, or payment details.

Limited Technical Data

To keep the app running smoothly, minimal technical information may be collected automatically. This includes anonymous data such as device type, screen resolution, or crash reports. This data cannot be used to identify you.

Advertisements

The Silent Hour may display banner advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These services may use their own technologies (such as cookies or identifiers) to deliver ads.

We do not access, store, or control any data collected by advertisers.

Cookies & Tracking

This app itself does not use cookies or tracking scripts. Any tracking technologies present originate from third-party services and are governed by their respective privacy policies.

Children

This app is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Future Updates

Future versions of the app may introduce optional features such as user accounts, bookmarks, or messaging. If those features require data collection, this Privacy Policy will be updated clearly and in advance.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect app improvements or legal requirements. Any updates will appear on this page with a revised date.
