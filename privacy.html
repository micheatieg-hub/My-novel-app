Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 1, 2026

The Silent Hour is designed to provide readers with a focused and safe reading experience. We respect your privacy and aim to keep the app simple and transparent.

No Personal Information Collected

The app does not ask for accounts, logins, or registration. We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, passwords, or payment details.

Technical Data

Minimal technical information may be collected automatically to maintain app performance, such as device type, screen resolution, and anonymous crash reports. This data cannot identify individual users.

Advertisements

The app may display banner ads from third-party networks. These ads may show content appropriate for general audiences. We do not collect or store any personal information through these ads.

Recommended Age

This novel is suitable for readers aged 10 and above. Younger readers may use the app at their guardian's discretion.

Future Updates

Optional features, such as user accounts, messaging, or bookmarks, may be added in future updates. Any data collection associated with these features will be clearly communicated.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be revised as the app evolves or to meet legal requirements. Changes will be posted here with the updated revision date.
